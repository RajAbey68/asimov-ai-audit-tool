About This Tool

This ASIMOV AI Governance Audit Tool helps organizations assess their AI systems against multiple governance frameworks including:

  • NIST AI Risk Management Framework
  • EU AI Act
  • Secure Controls Framework (SCF)
  • And more...

The tool uses a unified framework approach to help you identify gaps in your AI governance program and prioritize remediation efforts.
